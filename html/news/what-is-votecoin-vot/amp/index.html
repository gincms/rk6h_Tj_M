Introduction to VoteCoin

VoteCoin (VOT) is a equihash coin designed for the purpose of decentralized voting. It can be used for a variety of applications including: public elections, polls, crowd-funding and even betting.

VoteCoin's technology

VoteCoin is a fork of ZCash (ZEC) and inherits the equihash algorithm, along with it's (possibly fading) benefits. It also uses zk-SNARKS, which is the technology that allows for anonymous voting in elections and polls.

VoteCoin's latest developments

GUI wallet v1.0.0-rc1 release candidate was made available in March 2018 for Winows, OS X and Linux. Along side being able to send and receive transactions, it also includes a Vote section which can be used to anonymously participate in polls and betting.

VoteCoin team

The VoteCoin core team is made up of three developers (the lead being Tomas Matejicek, author of the pocket-sized Linux distro, Slax), and a designer. They seem to be rather elusive and don't seem to converse directly with the community much (if at all) on the Discord, which is a shame. @thesudio , who seems to be the Discord community manager and admin as of April 2018, appears to be a proxy to the development team, so there is indirect access to them, at least.

VoteCoin community

The VoteCoin Discord group isn't the most active, but since @thesudio took control of the community activity has increased. It has also become a lot more organised.

Thoughts on VoteCoin

VoteCoin has great potential. It's one of only a couple of established decentralised voting platforms in the crypto space (another being Decision Token from Horizon State). It's definitely feasible and even likely that there'll be wider-spread adoption of decentralised voting platforms to ensure verifiable and incorruptible voting. Of course, the qualities that make VoteCoin appealing to some will make it unappealing to others. But its support for betting significantly broadens the possibilities for this coin beyond polls and elections, so it will be very interesting to see where its journey leads.
