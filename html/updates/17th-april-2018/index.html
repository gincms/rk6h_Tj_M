Let's talk about transparency

Humans are often afraid to be honest because they fear the repercussions: being attacked or ridiculed; being judged; being wrong. But one of the beautiful things about cryptocurrencies is that they allow everyone to see what's happening. In other words: there's a degree of transparency that you don't get with fiat currencies.

Mining pools often claim to be transparent too, because they allow miners to see the coins paid out. But this isn't transparency, it's convenience. We're trying to do something a little different with LamboMoon: provide understanding of the fee structure – the why behind it – and evidence that what is advertised on the site is the fee that is actually taken. This is why a new tab has been added to the miners page: Earnings.

Here you'll see the dollar value of the coins paid out to you, the miners, each month, the value of the fee taken from the block reward, as well as the monthly cost of running the pool. The hope is that this, along with the pool fee info, goes a way to establishing more understanding and trust in LamboMoon, as well as encouraging other pools to offer a similar level of transparency, so that miners don't get caught out by scams.

To learn more about how the new tab and its calculations visit the pool transparency page.
