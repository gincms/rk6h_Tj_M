Fees

LamboMoon has a variable mining fee that decreases from 5% to 1% as the number miners/hashing power increases. This is to ensure that the costs of running the pool can be met and contributions can be made to the coin communities, while keeping miners' rewards as high as possible.

Remember that mining on a pool with fewer miners (such as LamboMoon) means that you earn more because you share the block rewards with fewer people.
